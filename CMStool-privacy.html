Privacy Policy

CMS Copyright Management Tool · Operated by YouBridge Media Technology (Beijing) Co., Ltd. (also known as "YouBridge Media" / "YB Media")
Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how YouBridge Media Technology (Beijing) Co., Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the CMS Copyright Management Tool (the "Application") — an internal desktop application used by our authorized staff to operate our YouTube Content Management System (CMS).

1. YouTube API Services

The Application uses YouTube API Services. By using the Application you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service. Our handling of information obtained through these APIs also adheres to the Google Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Access and Collect

Through Google/YouTube authorization granted by an administrator of our Content Owner account via OAuth (using the scopes youtube.upload, youtubepartner, and youtube.readonly), the Application accesses:

  • CMS assets, reference files, and copyright claims belonging to or managed by our Content Owner;
  • Content Owner information and claim / match policies;
  • Channel and video metadata (e.g., titles, IDs, durations) needed to create and review claims;
  • Asset labels and related metadata;
  • OAuth access / refresh tokens used to call the APIs on our behalf.

For access control, the Application also collects basic identity information from our enterprise DingTalk sign-in (union ID, nickname, avatar, and organization / department) and stores records of operations performed in the Application (e.g., upload, merge, and claim tasks).

The Application is used only with our own Content Owner account to manage content we own or are authorized to manage. It does not access data belonging to unrelated third-party users.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information solely to operate our YouTube CMS, including: uploading assets and reference files, creating and managing copyright claims, applying match policies, and reviewing potential claims. We do not use this information for advertising, and we do not sell it.

4. Storage and Security

Information is stored in our private company database (Alibaba Cloud RDS, PostgreSQL) with restricted access. Only authorized employees who have been approved by an administrator (via an allow-list) can use the Application. Access tokens are stored solely to maintain API access and are not shared.

5. Sharing

We do not share the information with third parties, except with Google / YouTube APIs as necessary to perform the functions described above. We do not sell or transfer the data.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information only as long as needed for our CMS operations. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the address below.

7. Revoking Access

You can revoke the Application's access to your Google / YouTube account at any time via the Google security settings page. Revoking access stops the Application from accessing YouTube data on your behalf.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request data deletion, contact: support@youbridges.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.

隐私政策

CMS 版权管理工具 · 运营方：优桥新媒体科技（北京）有限公司
最后更新：2026 年 6 月 21 日

本隐私政策说明优桥新媒体科技（北京）有限公司（以下称"我们"）在 CMS 版权管理工具（以下称"本应用"） 中如何处理相关信息。本应用是供我司授权员工使用的内部桌面应用，用于运营我司的 YouTube 内容管理系统（CMS）。

1. YouTube API 服务

本应用使用 YouTube API 服务（YouTube API Services）。使用本应用即表示你同意遵守 YouTube 服务条款； 我们对通过这些 API 获取信息的处理同样遵循 Google 隐私政策。

2. 我们访问与收集的信息

经我司 Content Owner（内容所有者）账号管理员通过 OAuth 授权（使用 youtube.upload、 youtubepartner、youtube.readonly 权限范围），本应用会访问：

  • 归属或由我司 Content Owner 管理的 CMS 资产、参考文件与版权主张；
  • Content Owner 信息及声权 / 匹配政策；
  • 创建与审核版权主张所需的频道、视频元数据（如标题、ID、时长）；
  • 资产标签及相关元数据；
  • 用于代表我司调用 API 的 OAuth 访问令牌 / 刷新令牌。

为进行访问控制，本应用还会收集来自企业钉钉登录的基础身份信息（unionId、昵称、头像、所属组织/部门）， 并记录在本应用内执行的操作（如上传、合并、声权任务）。

本应用仅用于我司自有 Content Owner 账号，管理我司拥有或获授权管理的内容，不会访问与之无关的第三方用户数据。

3. 信息的使用方式

我们仅将上述信息用于运营 YouTube CMS，包括：上传资产与参考文件、创建与管理版权主张、应用匹配政策、 审核潜在主张等。我们不将这些信息用于广告，不出售这些信息。

4. 存储与安全

信息存储于我司私有数据库（阿里云 RDS，PostgreSQL），访问受限。仅经管理员审核通过（白名单）的授权员工可使用本应用。 访问令牌仅用于维持 API 访问，不对外共享。

5. 信息共享

除为实现上述功能而必须与 Google / YouTube API 交互外，我们不会与第三方共享这些信息，也不会出售或转让数据。

6. 数据保留与删除

我们仅在 CMS 运营所需期间保留信息。你可通过下方联系方式申请删除你的数据。

7. 撤销授权

你可随时在 Google 安全设置页面 撤销本应用对你 Google / YouTube 账号的访问授权。撤销后，本应用将无法再代表你访问 YouTube 数据。

8. 联系方式

如对本隐私政策有疑问或需申请数据删除，请联系： support@youbridges.com。

9. 政策变更

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策，顶部"最后更新"日期为最新修订日期。
